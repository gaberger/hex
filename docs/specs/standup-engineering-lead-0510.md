Standup Engineering Lead Specification - May 10, 2024

This document specifies the structure and expectations for the engineering standup meeting on May 10, 2024.

## Purpose
To ensure alignment on progress, blockers, and next steps for the engineering team.

## Scope
- Covers all engineering team members
- Focuses on 24-hour progress and next steps
- Excludes non-engineering discussions

## Participants
- Engineering Lead (this document)
- All engineering team members (as defined in `teams/engineering-team.yaml`)
- Product Owner (for dependency alignment)

## Agenda
1. **Progress Updates** (5 minutes)
   - Each member shares 1-2 key accomplishments from the last 24 hours.
2. **Blockers** (10 minutes)
   - Identify and escalate any blockers preventing progress.
3. **Next Steps** (10 minutes)
   - Outline 1-2 tasks for the next 24 hours.
4. **Dependencies** (5 minutes)
   - Note cross-team dependencies requiring alignment.

## Next Steps
- Engineering Lead will review blockers and dependencies by 9:00 AM.
- All team members will confirm next steps by 9:30 AM.

## Blockers
- None reported as of 9:00 AM (to be updated by 9:30 AM).

## Dependencies
- Feature X requires Product Team approval (tracked in `projects/feature-x/dependencies.md`).

## Review
This spec will be reviewed by the Engineering Lead and Product Owner by 10:00 AM.
