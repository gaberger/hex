# ADR-2026-05-20-ic-responder-gap

## Status: Proposed

### Title

Investigate and Address IC Responder Gap

### Date

2026-05-20

### Authors

CTO

### Context

The current system for identifying and responding to incidents (IC) has gaps that need to be addressed. These gaps could lead to inefficiencies in response times, incomplete incident documentation, and potential operational risks.

### Decision

To address the identified gaps, we will implement a new incident responder framework that includes:

1. **Automated Detection**: Utilize machine learning algorithms to detect incidents more accurately and promptly.
2. **Improved Documentation**: Enhance the system for logging incident details to ensure completeness and traceability.
3. **Training Programs**: Develop training programs for staff to improve their incident response skills.

### Consequences

- **Enhanced Response Time**: Faster identification of incidents will allow for quicker responses, reducing potential downtime.
- **Better Incident Tracking**: Improved documentation will make it easier to track the progression and resolution of incidents.
- **Reduced Operational Risks**: A well-trained team will be better equipped to handle unexpected situations effectively.

### Next Steps

1. **Prototype Development**: Start developing a prototype for the automated detection system.
2. **Training Curriculum**: Begin creating training materials for incident responders.
3. **Pilot Testing**: Conduct pilot tests in selected areas of the organization to evaluate the effectiveness of the new framework.
